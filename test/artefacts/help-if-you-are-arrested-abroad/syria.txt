<div role="note" aria-label="Help" class="application-notice help-notice">
<p>There are no British consular services in Syria.  British nationals who need help should contact an embassy from any EU state in Damascus.</p>
</div>

<p>In emergency you can also call:</p>

<div class="contact">
<p>The Foreign and Commonwealth Office in London
+44 (0)20 7008 1500</p>
</div>

<p>Read more about how the FCO can help someone arrested abroad.</p>

<ul>
  <li><a href="/government/publications/support-for-british-nationals-abroad-a-guide">Support for British nationals</a></li>
  <li><a href="/government/publications/arrest-or-detention">In prison abroad</a></li>
</ul>

<ul>

</ul>
