<h2 id="doctors-letter-medical">Doctor’s letter (medical)</h2>
<p>Your doctor’s letter must be signed by a <a rel="external" href="http://www.gmc-uk.org/doctors/register/LRMP.asp">doctor registered with the General Medical Council.</a></p>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>Your document can’t be legalised if it has been signed on behalf of your doctor by another doctor or member of staff.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="get-your-documents-legalised">Get your documents legalised</h2>

<p>You can <a href="/get-document-legalised">get your documents legalised</a> by the Legalisation Office if they meet the standards above.</p>
