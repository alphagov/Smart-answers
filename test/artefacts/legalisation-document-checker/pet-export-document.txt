<h2 id="pet-export-document-from-defra">Pet export document from DEFRA</h2>
<p>Your pet export document can only be legalised if it has been signed and stamped by a veterinary surgeon registered with the Department of Food and Rural Affairs.</p>

<h2 id="get-your-documents-legalised">Get your documents legalised</h2>

<p>You can <a href="/get-document-legalised">get your documents legalised</a> by the Legalisation Office if they meet the standards above.</p>
