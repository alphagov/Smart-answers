{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>Your State Pension age is 65 years.</p>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>The State Pension will change on 6 April 2016. You will claim the <a href="/new-state-pension">new State Pension</a> when you reach your State Pension age.</p>
</div>

<p>You’ll have to contact the Future Pension Centre to get an estimate of your pension calculated under the new State Pension rules.</p>

<h2 id="contact-the-future-pension-centre">Contact the Future Pension Centre</h2>

<p>You can call the Future Pension centre or <a href="/government/publications/application-for-a-state-pension-statement">download an application form</a>. The address is on the form.</p>
