<h2 id="how-long-it-takes">How long it takes</h2>

<p>Your application will take <strong>at least</strong> 8 weeks from when it’s received by Her Majesty&rsquo;s Passport Office in the UK.</p>

<p>If you haven’t already reported the loss or theft, you must include <a href="/government/publications/lost-or-stolen-passport-notification--2">form LS01</a> with your declaration form.</p>

<p>You may have to attend an interview.</p>

<p>Applications will take longer if:</p>

<ul>
  <li>HM Passport Office need to ask you for more information or documents</li>
  <li>the photographs you send are rejected</li>
</ul>

<p>Don’t book any travel until you have a valid passport. If you need to travel more urgently, you may be able to apply for an <a href="/emergency-travel-document">Emergency Travel Document</a>.</p>

<h2 id="cost">Cost</h2>

<p>You’ll have to pay a fee for your passport and a courier fee of £9.70. The courier fee pays for your passport to be sent back to you securely.</p>

<h2 id="how-to-apply">How to apply</h2>

<p>You must apply and pay for your passport online.</p>

<p>Before you start you need:</p>

<ul>
  <li>2 identical new photos of you (or your child, if it’s a child passport application)</li>
  <li>any other current passports issued by other countries</li>
  <li>a MasterCard, Visa, Visa Electron, Visa Debit or Maestro (UK Domestic) card - Maestro (International) cards aren’t accepted</li>
</ul>

<p>Read the <a href="/government/publications/help-completing-the-online-passport-application-from-outside-the-uk">guidance notes</a> to help you fill in your online application. Check which <a href="/government/publications/overseas-passport-applications-supporting-documents-guidance">supporting documents</a> you must send with your application. Any documents that aren’t in English (including documents showing an address) must be translated by a professional translator.</p>

<p>You will need to print, sign and post your declaration form at the end.</p>

<p><a rel="external" href="https://passportapplication.service.gov.uk/">Start your application on Her Majesty’s Passport Office website</a></p>

<h2 id="getting-your-passport">Getting your passport</h2>

<p>Your passport and supporting documents will be delivered separately by courier.</p>
