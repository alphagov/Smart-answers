<h2 id="statutory-maternity-leave">Statutory Maternity Leave</h2>

<p>The employee is not entitled to Statutory Maternity Leave because they don’t have an employment contract with you.</p>

<p>Write to them within 28 days of their leave request confirming this.</p>

<h2 id="statutory-maternity-pay">Statutory Maternity Pay</h2>

<p>The employee is entitled to up to 39 weeks Statutory Maternity Pay (<abbr title="Statutory Maternity Pay">SMP</abbr>) if they <a href="/maternity-leave-pay-employees/notice-period" title="Notice period">claim <abbr title="Statutory Maternity Pay">SMP</abbr> in time</a> and give <a href="/maternity-leave-pay-employees/eligibility" title="Proof of pregnancy">proof of the pregnancy</a>.</p>
