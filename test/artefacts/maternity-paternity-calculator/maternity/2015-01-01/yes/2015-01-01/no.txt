<h2 id="statutory-maternity-leave">Statutory Maternity Leave</h2>

<h2 id="statutory-maternity-pay">Statutory maternity pay</h2>

<p>The employee is not entitled to statutory maternity pay. To qualify:</p>

<ul>
  <li>they must have started working for you on or before 29 March 2014</li>
</ul>

<p>You must write confirming this within 28 days of their maternity pay request.</p>

<p>Send them form SMP1 within 7 days of your decision. They must get this form within 28 days of their pay request or the birth (whichever is earlier).</p>
