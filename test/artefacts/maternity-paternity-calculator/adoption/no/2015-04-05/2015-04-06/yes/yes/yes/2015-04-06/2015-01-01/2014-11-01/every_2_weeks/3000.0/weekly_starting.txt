<h2 id="statutory-adoption-pay">Statutory Adoption Pay</h2>

<p>The employee is entitled to up to 39 weeks Statutory Adoption Pay (SAP) if they <a href="/employers-adoption-pay-leave/notice-period">claim SAP in time</a> and give you <a href="/employers-adoption-pay-leave/proof-of-adoption">proof of the adoption</a>.</p>

<p>The employee’s average weekly earnings are: £375.00.</p>
