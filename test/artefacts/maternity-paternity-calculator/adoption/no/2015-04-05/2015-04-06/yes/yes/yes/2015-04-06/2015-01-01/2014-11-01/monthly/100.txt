<h2 id="statutory-adoption-leave">Statutory Adoption Leave</h2>

<p>The employee is entitled to up to 52 weeks Statutory Adoption Leave if they <a href="/employers-adoption-pay-leave/notice-period">claim the leave on time</a>.</p>

  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Leave</th>
      <th>Key dates</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>Start</td>
      <td>6 April 2015</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>End</td>
      <td>3 April 2016</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

<h2 id="statutory-adoption-pay">Statutory Adoption Pay</h2>

<p>The employee is not entitled to Statutory Adoption Pay. To qualify:</p>

<ul>
  <li>their average weekly earnings (£11.54) between Sunday, 02 November 2014 and Thursday, 01 January 2015 must be at least £111.00</li>
</ul>

<p>Send them form SAP1 within 7 days of your decision. They must get this form within 28 days of their pay request or the date they were matched with the child (whichever is earlier).</p>
