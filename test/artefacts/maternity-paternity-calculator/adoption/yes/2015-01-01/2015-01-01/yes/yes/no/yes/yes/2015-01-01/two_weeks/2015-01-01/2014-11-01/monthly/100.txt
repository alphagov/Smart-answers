<h2 id="statutory-paternity-leave">Statutory Paternity Leave</h2>

<p>The employee is not entitled to Statutory Paternity Leave because they don’t have an employment contract with you.</p>

<p>Write to them within 28 days of their leave request confirming this.</p>

<h2 id="statutory-paternity-pay">Statutory Paternity Pay</h2>

<p>The employee is not entitled to Statutory Paternity Pay. To qualify:</p>

<ul>
  <li>their average weekly earnings (£11.54) between Sunday, 02 November 2014 and Thursday, 01 January 2015 must be at least £111.00</li>
</ul>

<p>Send them form OSPP1 confirming this within 28 days of their pay request.</p>
