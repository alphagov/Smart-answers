

Your child maintenance payment is the reduced rate of £7 per week.

^This is an estimate. The exact amount the Child Maintenance Service calculates may be different.^

For example, if the paying parent also supports other children, the amount you receive may be less than this.

## Fees

Payment type | Weekly payment | Fees | Total amount you’ll receive | Application fee  - you only pay this once
- | -
[Collect and Pay](/child-maintenance/how-to-pay) | £7 | 28p | £6.72 | £20.00
[Direct pay](/child-maintenance/how-to-pay) | £7 | No fees | £7 | £20.00
[Arranging child maintenance yourself](/arranging-child-maintenance-yourself) | £7 | No fees | £7 | No fee

You will pay up to £14.56 in fees per year if you receive child maintenance through the Collect and Pay service.

^You don’t have to pay an application fee if you’re in Northern Ireland.^

Read more about [how child maintenance is worked out](/how-child-maintenance-is-worked-out/child-maintenance-rates).
