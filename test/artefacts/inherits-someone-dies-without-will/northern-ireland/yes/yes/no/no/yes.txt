{::comment}begin-title{::/comment}
The husband, wife or civil partner keeps all the assets (including property) up to £450,000, and all the personal possessions, whatever their value.{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>The husband, wife or civil partner must survive the deceased by at least 28 days to inherit.</p>

<p>They also get half the amount left over.</p>

<p>The remainder is divided between any brothers or sisters.</p>

<p>If any brother or sister is dead, their children (the deceased’s nieces and nephews) inherit.</p>
{::comment}end-body{::/comment}
