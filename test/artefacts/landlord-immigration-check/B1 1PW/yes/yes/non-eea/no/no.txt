Does the person have have a Home Office document marked ‘exempt from immigration control’, ‘no time limit’ or ‘right of abode’?

This can be any of the following:

- a biometric ‘residence permit’ card
- a Permanent Residence Card, indefinite leave to remain, indefinite leave to enter or no time limit card issued by the Home Office
- a ‘certificate of entitlement’ in their foreign passport
- a ‘travel document' or ‘immigration status document’
- an ‘exempt vignette’ (sticker in their foreign passport) - it could also be marked ‘official’ or ‘diplomatic’




  * yes: Yes
  * no: No
