

You don’t have to make a right to rent check at this time.

^All landlords in England will have to [make right to rent checks](/check-tenant-right-to-rent-documents) from 1 February 2016.^

You can read the landlord’s [code of practice](/government/publications/right-to-rent-landlords-code-of-practice) on making checks for more information.

You can also [register for updates](https://www.qbaseprojects.co.uk/homeoffice/survey.asp?id=27) about the right to rent check and when it'll apply across the UK.
