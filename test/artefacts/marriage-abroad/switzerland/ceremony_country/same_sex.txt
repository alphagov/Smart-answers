Civil partnership in Switzerland
{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>Same-sex relationships in Switzerland that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as ’eingetragene Partnerschaft’, ’partenariat enregistré’, ’unione domestica registrata’ or ’partenadi registrà’ (’registered partnership’).</p>

<p>Contact the Civil Register Office where you’re entering into a civil partnership to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>
