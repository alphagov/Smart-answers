Civil partnership in Mexico{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>Same-sex relationships in Mexico that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as:</p>

<ul>
  <li>‘pacto civil de solidaridad’, or ‘civil solidarity pact’ (in Coahuila)</li>
  <li>‘sociedad de convivencia’, or ‘domestic partnership’ (in Mexico City Federal District)</li>
  <li>marriage (in Mexico City Federal District)</li>
</ul>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>You should <a href="/government/collections/list-of-lawyers">get legal advice</a> before making any plans.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="what-you-need-to-do">What you need to do</h2>

<p>Contact the relevant local authorities in Mexico to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<p>There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a same-sex relationship, or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible enter into a same-sex relationship, in Mexico.</p>

<h2 id="naturalisation-of-your-partner-if-they-move-to-the-uk">Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK</h2>

<p>Your partner can apply to <a href="/becoming-a-british-citizen/if-your-spouse-is-a-british-citizen">become a British citizen</a> once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.</p>
