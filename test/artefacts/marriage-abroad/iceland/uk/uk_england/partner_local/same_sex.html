Getting married abroad

Civil partnership in Iceland

Same-sex relationships in Iceland that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as:

  • marriage
  • ’staðfesta samvist’, or ’confirmed cohabitation’

Contact the Embassy of Iceland before making any plans to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.

You should also check the travel advice for Iceland.

What you need to do

You may be asked to provide a certificate of no impediment (CNI) or a similar document to prove you’re allowed to enter into a civil partnership or equivalent in Iceland.

Contact the local British embassy or consulate where you’re planning the ceremony to find out what you need to do.

Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to become a British citizen once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
