Civil partnership in Argentina{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>Same-sex relationships in Argentina that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as:</p>

<ul>
  <li>marriage</li>
  <li>‘unión civil’, or ‘civil union’</li>
</ul>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>You should <a href="/government/collections/list-of-lawyers">get legal advice</a> and check the <a href="/foreign-travel-advice/argentina">travel advice for Argentina</a> before making any plans.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="what-you-need-to-do">What you need to do</h2>

<p>Contact your <a href="/government/publications/foreign-embassies-in-the-uk">nearest embassy or consulate</a> representing Argentina before making any plans to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<p>There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a same-sex relationship, or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible enter into a same-sex relationship, in Argentina.</p>
