Marriage in Slovakia{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>Contact your <a href="/government/publications/foreign-embassies-in-the-uk">nearest embassy or consulate</a> representing Slovakia before making any plans to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>You should <a href="/government/collections/list-of-lawyers">get legal advice</a> and check the <a href="/foreign-travel-advice/slovakia">travel advice for Slovakia</a> before making any plans.</p>
</div>

<p>There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in Slovakia.</p>
