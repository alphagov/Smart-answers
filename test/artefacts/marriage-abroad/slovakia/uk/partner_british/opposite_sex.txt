Marriage in Slovakia

Contact your [nearest embassy or consulate](/government/publications/foreign-embassies-in-the-uk) representing Slovakia before making any plans to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.


^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) and check the [travel advice for Slovakia](/foreign-travel-advice/slovakia) before making any plans.


There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in Slovakia.


*[GRO]:General Register Office
*[FCO]:Foreign &amp; Commonwealth Office
