<p>Contact the relevant local authorities in Costa Rica to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<p>You may be asked for a certificate of no impediment (CNI) or similar document. Check with the authorities in <a rel="external" href="http://www.gov.je/pages/contacts.aspx?contactId=71">Jersey</a> or <a rel="external" href="http://www.guernseyroyalcourt.gg/article/1663/Contact-Us">Guernsey</a> to find out what they can provide, then contact the authorities in Costa Rica to confirm if this is acceptable.</p>

<p>The local British embassy or consulate may be able to advise if you have any problems getting the documents you need from the authorities in Jersey or Guernsey.</p>
