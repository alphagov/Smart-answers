{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>Bonaire St Eustatius Saba is one of the Dutch Caribbean islands.</p>

<p>Contact the relevant local authorities in Bonaire St Eustatius Saba to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">

</div>

<p>There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in Bonaire St Eustatius Saba.</p>
