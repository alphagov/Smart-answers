Getting married abroad

Marriage in the Czech Republic

Contact your nearest embassy or consulate representing the Czech Republic before making any plans to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.

You should get legal advice and check the travel advice for the Czech Republic before making any plans.

There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in the Czech Republic.

Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to become a British citizen once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
