Civil partnership in the Czech Republic{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>Same-sex relationships in the Czech Republic that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as ’registrované partnerství’, or ‘registered partnership’.</p>

<p>Contact the relevant local authorities in the Czech Republic to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<h2 id="naturalisation-of-your-partner-if-they-move-to-the-uk">Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK</h2>

<p>Your partner can apply to <a href="/becoming-a-british-citizen/if-your-spouse-is-a-british-citizen">become a British citizen</a> once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.</p>
