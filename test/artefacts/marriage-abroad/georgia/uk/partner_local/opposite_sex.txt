Marriage in Georgia

Contact the [Ministry of Justice of Georgia](http://www.sda.gov.ge/?page_id=7303&lang=en) to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.

^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) and check the [travel advice for Georgia](/foreign-travel-advice/georgia) before making any plans.

##Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to [become a British citizen](/becoming-a-british-citizen/if-your-spouse-is-a-british-citizen) once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
