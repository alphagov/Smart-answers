Same-sex marriage and civil partnership in New Zealand{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>You may be able to get married or enter into a ‘civil union’ at the British embassy or consulate in New Zealand. Civil unions are recognised as civil partnerships under British law.</p>

<p>Contact the <a href="/government/publications/foreign-embassies-in-the-uk">High Commission of New Zealand</a> to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>You should <a href="/government/collections/list-of-lawyers">get legal advice</a> and check the <a href="/foreign-travel-advice/new-zealand">travel advice for New Zealand</a> before making any plans.</p>
</div>
