Getting married abroad

Civil partnership in Austria

Same-sex relationships in Austria that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as ‘eingetragene Partnerschaft’, or ‘registered partnership’.

Contact the relevant local authorities in Austria to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.

You should also check the travel advice for Austria.

What you need to do

You may be asked to provide a certificate of no impediment (CNI) or a similar document to prove you’re allowed to enter into a civil partnership or equivalent in Austria.

Contact the local British embassy or consulate where you’re planning the ceremony to find out what you need to do.

Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to become a British citizen once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.

Fees

You can pay by cash or credit card, but not by personal cheque.
