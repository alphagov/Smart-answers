Civil partnership in the USA{::comment}end-title{::/comment}

{::comment}begin-body{::/comment}
<p>You can get a same-sex relationship legally recognised in some states in the United States.</p>

<p>Same-sex relationships in US states that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as:</p>

<ul>
  <li>‘domestic partnership’ or ‘marriage’ (California)</li>
  <li>‘the relationship between designated beneficiaries’ (Colorado)</li>
  <li>‘civil union’ or ‘marriage’ (Connecticut)</li>
  <li>‘civil union’ (Delaware)</li>
  <li>‘marriage’ (District of Colombia)</li>
  <li>‘civil union’ or ‘reciprocal beneficiary relationship’ (Hawaii)</li>
  <li>‘civil union’ (Illinois)</li>
  <li>‘marriage’ (Iowa)</li>
  <li>‘domestic partnership’ (Maine)</li>
  <li>‘marriage’ (Massachusetts)</li>
  <li>‘domestic partnership’ (Nevada)</li>
  <li>‘marriage’ (New Hampshire)</li>
  <li>‘civil union’ or ‘domestic partnership’ (New Jersey)</li>
  <li>‘marriage’ (New York)</li>
  <li>‘domestic partnership’ (Oregon)</li>
  <li>‘civil union’ (Rhode Island)</li>
  <li>‘civil union’ or ‘marriage’ (Vermont)</li>
  <li>‘state registered domestic partnership’ (Washington)</li>
  <li>‘domestic partnership’ (Wisconsin)</li>
</ul>

<div role="note" aria-label="Information" class="application-notice info-notice">
<p>You should <a href="/government/collections/list-of-lawyers">get legal advice</a> and check the <a href="/foreign-travel-advice/usa">travel advice for the USA</a> before making any plans.</p>
</div>

<h2 id="what-you-need-to-do">What you need to do</h2>

<p>Contact the relevant local authorities in the USA to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<p>There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a same-sex relationship, or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible enter into a same-sex relationship, in the USA.</p>
