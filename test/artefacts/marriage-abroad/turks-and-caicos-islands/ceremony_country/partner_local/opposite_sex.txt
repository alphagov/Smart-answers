Marriage in the Turks And Caicos Islands

The Turks And Caicos Islands is a British overseas territory.

Contact the Turks And Caicos Islands Government to find out about getting married there, including what documents you’ll need.

##Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to [become a British citizen](/becoming-a-british-citizen/if-your-spouse-is-a-british-citizen) once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
