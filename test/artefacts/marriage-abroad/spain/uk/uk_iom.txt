<p>Contact the relevant local authorities in Spain to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.</p>

<div role="note" aria-label="Help" class="application-notice help-notice">
<p>There are certain legal restrictions if neither of you is resident in Spain. You should check with the <a rel="external" href="http://www.mjusticia.gob.es/BUSCADIR/ServletControlador?apartado=buscadorGeneral&amp;tipo=RC">civil registry</a> where you’re planning to get married to find out if this is allowed.</p>
</div>

<p>You may be asked for a certificate of no impediment (CNI) or similar document. Check with the authorities in the <a rel="external" href="http://www.gov.im/registries/general/civilregistry/">Isle of Man</a> to find out what they can provide, then contact the authorities in Spain to confirm if this is acceptable.</p>

<p>The local British embassy or consulate may be able to advise if you have any problems getting the documents you need from the Manx authorities.</p>
